MEET THE COUPLE

Dr Joseph

Dr. Joseph Etuk was born in Ikoyi Lagos. His first school leaving and secondary school certificate was attained in Army children school and united Christian secondary school Apapa Lagos. He secured admission into the university of uyo in 2009, to study Medicine and Surgery where became the chairman of the largest hostel in University of Uyo (M2) in 2012/13. Following afterwards, He led the students in the capacity of the SUG president in 2015/16 session. He is currently a medical Doctor and a legitimate business man in practice.

Esther

Esther was born and raised in Lagos, to a family of 6. After her secondary school, she moved to Akwa-ibom state to attend the University of Uyo, where her passion for philosophy was born. After 4 years in the university of Uyo, she graduated with a degree in Philosophy. Afterwards, she moved to Ibadan, Oyo state in 2017, where she had her NYSC. She works in a Financial institution, she is a lover of good things and a go - getter.
